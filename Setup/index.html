General Setup Guide for SYSbot

This (very) simple guide will walk you through SYSbot setup.
SYSbot has only one (1) way of knowing Admins from other users; Roles.
SYSbot will only allow users with a role named "Admin" (caution capital A) to execute administrative commands.
So, how do you set up SYSbot?

  1. Create a Role named "Admin" (if you dont already have one)
  2. Give it to Admins.
  3. Done! Now SYSbot will listen to what Admins say separate from what normal users say.
